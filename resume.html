  • Le Monde • 09/2020 - Present

    I am currently working for Le Monde as a journalist and developer apprentice. As a part of Les Décodeurs' team, my main missions are crafting data-driven articles, and developing a d3 map generator for all journalists.

  • Le Monde • 06/2020 - 07/2020

    As an intern for Les Décodeurs, I contributed to the creation of datavisualizations and data-driven articles. This internship allowed me to be hired as an apprentice at Les Décodeurs for my last year of Master's degree.

  • RTL • 05/2019 - 07/2019

    Winning the François Chalais's young reporter award for radio granted me the opportunity to intern at RTL (first or second radio in France depending on ranking metrics) for three months. I joined the reporting team and produced a wide variety of radio formats that were aired during RTL's news segments. I learned how to use Netia (editing software), Nagra Arès C (recording device) and Scoopfones (for live reporting). I was also given some thrilling assistant missions: I wrote anchorwoman Elizabeth Martichoux's notes for a European elections special edition debate she mediated; I served as Stéphane Bern's sparring partner to prepare and record his summer chronicles...

  • Le Figaro • 05/2018 - 08/2018

    As a part of the new formats team, I was in charge of writing and editing interactive stories for Le Figaro.fr’s mobile app. I had the opportunity to learn using After Effects.

  • Festi.tv • 07/2017

    Festi.tv is an online TV that covers the Avignon Festival (press accreditation for both In and Off festivals). As a culture journalist for Festi TV, I attended up to four drama plays a day and reviewing them, reported on various events related to the festival, and on In and Off shows including interviews of the artists, and hosted interviews and debates with directors and comedians for Festi TV's shows.

  • L'Est Républicain • 06/2017

    I wrote various types of articles for l'Est Républicain's daily local edition Doubs/Haut-Doubs (print newspapers and online).

  • La Presse de Vesoul • 12/2016

    I reported on various stories, collected local political opinion data, took photographies, and published articles online and on the weekly newspapers.
